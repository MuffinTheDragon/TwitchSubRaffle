What is it?

This is a simple website where you can connect with your twitch account, and pick a random winner from all your subscribers.

How does it work?

The website makes requests to the Twitch API to get a user's ID and list of subscribers. It then pseudo-randomly picks a winner.

FAQ

1. If you are automatically redirected to this page, it most likely means your access token is invalid. Try clicking the link at the bottom of this page again.

2. If you want to remove this app's access from your Twitch account. Login to your Twitch->Settings->Connections->Disconnect Subcriber Raffle Picker.
